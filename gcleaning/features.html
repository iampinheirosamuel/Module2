Green Cleaning Service

In Green Cleaning Service, we offer you the best of biodegradable cleaning. We understand the condition of your environment and the need to protect your health against adverse effect using unfriendly cleaning agent for your home, office and environment. Green cleaning techniques and products avoid the use of chemically reactive and toxic products which contain various toxic chemicals, some of which emit volatile organic compounds causing respiratory, dermatological and other conditions. It is behavioral as well as simply using healthy and environmentally friendly products. In fact, more emphasis these days is being placed on microfiber technology which eliminates the need for chemical cleaning supplies or even green cleaners. Just water alone and a quality microfiber cloth can eliminate over 99% of bacteria from hard surfaces.

Our clients can choose any of the cleaning options whether weekly, daily, bi-weekly, monthly cleaning plan that satisfies their taste.

Fumigation and Pest Control Service

In Fumigation and Pest Control Service, we make use of the best fumigants to suffocate or poison the pests within the building. Firstly the area intended to be fumigated is usually covered to create a sealed environment; next the fumigant is released into the space to be fumigated; then the space is held for a set period while the funigant percolates through the space and acts on and kills any infestation in the product, next the space is ventilated so that the fumigants are allowed to escape from the space, and render it safe for humans to enter and your building is free from pests!
