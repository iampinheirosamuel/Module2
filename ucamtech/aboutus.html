About Us

UCAMTECH Elementary School

This is a subsidiary of UCAMTECH, it is the Elementary school where pupils are trained-up and nurtured with the basic educational knowledge(Universal Basic Education) for nursery, primary and secondary school students.

We provide the pleasant environment for learning to impart students sufficiently for knowledge acquisition morally, spiritually, academically and socially .

  • The School management

The activities of the school is being overseen by Mr & Mrs Hamed who are the founder of UCAMTECH Elementary School.

 

Mr Olufemi Hamed( Bsc. Msc. OAU)

He is the founder of UCAMTECH and also the Director of Studies of the Satelite Campus of Gateway Polytechnic, Igbesa Ogun State. He holds a Bachelor's Degree in Electronics and Electrical Engineering from the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University Ile-ife, Nigeria. He holds a Master's Degree in the same field from the same University.
