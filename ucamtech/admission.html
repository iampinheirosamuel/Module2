Admission

You are welcome to UCAMTECH Elementary School

We are pleased to have you enrol with us this session.

Please follow the seven(7) steps listed below.

  • Step 1

    • Purchase admission forms sold at the school.

  • Step 2

    • Register online providing your biodata information.

  • Step

    • Entrance exams comes up at a stipulated date that will be communicated to you later.

  • Step 4

    • If you miss the entrance exam, you would have to write a placement test.

  • Step 5

    • Collect result on a set date.

  • Step 6

    • Collect admission letter if successful with a down payment to secure a place otherwise you might forfeit the admission after the closing date.

  • Step 7

    • Resume with a photocopy of the following;

    1. medical certificate
    2. birth certificate
    3. receipt or teller of payments
    4. completed and signed under taking form
