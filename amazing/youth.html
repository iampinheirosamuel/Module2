Youth Empowerment

This month we would be introducing the concept of Youth Empowerment in general and our perspectives about it. You can get back to us if you have questions or contributions or you can discuss with other intellectuals on our blog.

Youth Empowerment is the process of building and moulding young minds into great men and women to create a better world in the future. There is more to learning and life in genral than the walls of schools and young minds should be trained for everything, not just the basic subjects in the curriculum. There isno exception in this as there is no boundary in knowledge, asides this prepares one to face life challenges and be different. The advantage of been empowered as a youth or empowering other youth is like a mustard seed sown, the fruit and benefit is incomparable to the input.

There is no specified or stereotyped way or system of empowering youth but there are definitely core values which it is made up of and they include the following. Developing the Right motive, Self-discovery, Personal Development, Developing Skills and Talents, Getting the best Education, Choosing the right Career, Providing Solutions to Problems, Entrepreneurship, Discovering Resource Portals, Mentoring, Making your Dreams come to reality, Gaining Stability and lots more. We would be looking into this in full in sucsequent months. It would be of great value to follow up this course and there is an opportunity to interact with people of like minds.
