Welcome to Royal Youth Academy

By Admin posted March 30, 2017

On behalf of the school management, staff, students, PTA, friends and well wishers, I wish to welcome you to Royal Youth Academy, Igwo-Obudu. It is our honest desire to establish a school that will provide the educational needs of a teeming populace who would be excluded otherwise.

We encourage all and sundry to enroll their wards in order to have real value for whatever they will spend. It is our hope that passing through this institution of learning will provide the needed training that will make syudents global citizens. They will be able to meet and interact with their counterparts from around the globe.

You are once  again welcome to Royal Youth Academy.
