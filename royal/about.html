Royal Youth Academy, Igwo-Obudu

 Royal Youth Academy is a private secondary school established on September 2007 by the vibrant young man called Dr Ugal David Betluwhobel (proprietor). The school at inception had nine (9) staffs and fourty-four (44) students. The pioneer principal was Mr Joseph Adie Allah.

The school was established with a vision to provide quality education to children from rural and urban areas who would have excluded otherwise, it is also for children who's parents have no money to educate their wards in the urban area. To also evaluate the student's learning abilities after graduation. Moreso, to create oportunities through various subjects taught for students to fit different professional career.

The school is located in Igwo-Village along Ogoja road about sixty kilometres (60km) from Obudu town. It is a mixed school operating in its permanent site. The school has Government approval to operate.

School Curriculum

The school offers subjects across different areas of specialization such as Sciences, Social Siciences, Arts as well as Commercial subjects. Foreign language subjects are also taught. 

Staffing

The school has employed quality teachers in different areas of specialization to offer the best training to the children so to become functioning members of the society tomorrow. We are hiring.

Examination

The school always examine the students to ascertain their comprehensive ability. The school embark on formatie and summative evaluation of her academic progress. All the students are expected to write their examinations ans core seventy percent (70%) which will be added to thrity percent (30%) test to make up hundred percent (100%) in all the subjects. The school authority ensure that on no account should any student be involve in examination malpractice.
